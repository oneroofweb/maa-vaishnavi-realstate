About Maa Vaishnavi Construction

Lucknow is a city of culture, celebration, and royal charm. Known as the City of Nawabs, Lucknow has always loved grand gatherings, music, food, and beautiful decorations. From traditional weddings to modern birthday parties, from corporate meetings to social get-togethers, events are a big part of life here.

If you are searching online for any kind of event service in Lucknow — whether it is a banquet hall, wedding planner, event decorator, photographer, caterer, DJ, anchor, or surprise party planner — this platform is made for you.

Events Bucket is an event directory website created especially for the people of Lucknow. Our goal is simple: We connect you with the right event service providers in the city. Instead of searching on many different websites, calling random numbers, and wasting time, you can find everything related to events in one place. We list trusted and professional event planners, wedding services, banquet halls, decorators, photographers, caterers, entertainers, and many other vendors who help you create beautiful memories.

Weddings, engagements, birthdays, corporate launches, school functions, political programs, exhibitions, kitty parties, anniversary celebrations, baby showers, and religious functions — the list never ends. Because of this high demand, many event companies and vendors are working in the city. But finding the right one can be confusing.
